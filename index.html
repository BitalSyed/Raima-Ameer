Fix-It-Now Platform

Fix It Now is a robust platform developed to connect users with service providers for quick and efficient repairs. The platform was designed with both clients and service professionals in mind, offering an intuitive and easy-to-use interface for seamless interactions.

  • Service Search Engine: Users can quickly find repair services in their area using a real-time search engine that filters by category and location.
  • Worker Sign-up Portal: Service providers can sign up, create profiles, and list their expertise, making it easy for users to choose the right person for the job.

  • Admin Dashboard: A comprehensive admin panel for monitoring all activities on the platform, from job requests to worker performance.
